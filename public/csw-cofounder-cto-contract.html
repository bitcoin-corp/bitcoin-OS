1. APPOINTMENT

1.1 The Company hereby appoints the Executive as:

(a) Co-Founder of The Bitcoin Corporation Limited

(b) Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

(c) Director of the Company

1.2 The appointment shall commence on __________ 2025 (the "Commencement Date") and shall continue, subject to the provisions of this Agreement, until terminated by either party.

1.3 This appointment is subject to the Executive's acceptance of the terms and conditions set out in this Offer.

2. EQUITY OWNERSHIP

2.1 Share Allocation

The Executive shall be granted 65% (sixty-five percent) of the total issued share capital of the Company, to be issued upon execution of this Agreement.

2.2 Vesting Schedule

(a) 50% of shares vest immediately upon signing

(b) Remaining 50% vest upon the earlier of: (i) 12 months from commencement, or (ii) achievement of £10 million annual revenue run-rate

(c) Full acceleration upon successful establishment of the Bitcoin Development Trust

2.3 Share Rights

The shares shall carry full voting rights and shall rank pari passu with all other ordinary shares in the Company.

2.4 Trust Issuance Option

The shares may be issued to a discretionary trust settled by the Executive, provided the Executive maintains full control and beneficial ownership of such trust.

3. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1 Primary Responsibilities

The Executive shall have substantial autonomy in defining the technical direction and priorities of the Company, with core responsibilities including but not limited to:

(a) Technical Strategy: Define and implement the overall technical architecture for the Bitcoin OS ecosystem

(b) Development Priorities: Determine which applications and tools to prioritize for development

(c) Standards Setting: Establish technical standards and best practices across all platform components

(d) Innovation Leadership: Direct research and development of new features and capabilities

(e) Protocol Implementation: Oversee Bitcoin protocol integration and blockchain architecture

(f) Quality Assurance: Ensure all products meet professional standards for reliability and usability

3.2 Flexible Focus Areas

The Executive shall have discretion to allocate time and resources across:

(a) Core infrastructure development

(b) Application ecosystem expansion

(c) Developer tools and documentation

(d) Smart contract and tokenization frameworks

(e) Integration with existing Bitcoin infrastructure

(f) Any other technical initiatives deemed strategic by the Executive

3.3 Collaborative Approach

The Executive shall work collaboratively with the CEO on strategic decisions while maintaining technical autonomy in implementation approaches.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 All intellectual property created by the Executive in the course of employment shall belong to the Company, subject to:

(a) Fair attribution and recognition of the Executive's contributions

(b) The Executive's right to use such IP for academic and research purposes

(c) Open source licensing where strategically appropriate

6.2 Pre-existing intellectual property of the Executive remains their property, with the Company granted a perpetual, royalty-free license for use in Company products.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 The Executive agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information, except:

(a) Information already in the public domain

(b) Information required to be disclosed by law

(c) Technical information intended for open-source release

8. TERMINATION

8.1 Notice Period

(a) By the Executive: 3 months' written notice

(b) By the Company: 6 months' written notice

8.2 Immediate Termination

The Company may terminate without notice only for gross misconduct as defined by UK employment law.

8.3 Post-Termination

Vested shares remain with the Executive. Unvested shares are forfeited unless termination is without cause.

9. TRUST STRUCTURE

9.1 Bitcoin Development Trust

(a) The Executive shall have direct control and trusteeship of the Bitcoin Development Trust upon its establishment

(b) Trust assets and intellectual property remain under Executive's technical direction

(c) Company operations and trust objectives shall be aligned under Executive's leadership

9.2 No Restrictions

The Executive faces no non-compete or non-solicitation restrictions, recognizing that:

(a) Bitcoin development requires open collaboration

(b) The Executive's expertise benefits the entire ecosystem

(c) Restrictive covenants would be contrary to Bitcoin's open-source philosophy

(a) Board representation rights

(b) Veto rights on fundamental changes to company structure

(c) Input on all strategic decisions

10.2 Major decisions requiring mutual agreement:

(a) Sale or merger of the Company

(b) Raising external investment

(c) Fundamental changes to business model
